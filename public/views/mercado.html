About Mercado

Mercado, located in the heart of Nairobi, is a vibrant restaurant that brings the authentic flavors of Mexico to the city. Renowned for its bold and flavorful dishes, Mercado's menu features an array of traditional Mexican favorites such as tacos, enchiladas, guacamole, and churros, all prepared with a modern twist and the freshest ingredients.

The restaurant's colorful and lively decor, inspired by the vibrant culture of Mexico, creates a festive and welcoming ambiance, perfect for social gatherings, family meals, or casual dining. Pair your meal with their signature margaritas or handcrafted cocktails, and Mercado guarantees a delightful culinary fiesta that transports you straight to the streets of Mexico City.

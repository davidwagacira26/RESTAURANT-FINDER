About Cafe Cassia

Artisan Blend, located along Mombasa Road, is a contemporary café and bistro that celebrates the art of exceptional coffee and flavorful cuisine. Known for its expertly brewed specialty coffee and thoughtfully crafted menu, this spot is a favorite among those seeking quality and creativity in every bite and sip.

With its sleek, modern interiors and a cozy atmosphere, Artisan Blend offers the perfect setting for casual meetups, business lunches, or a relaxing break from the day's hustle. Whether you're savoring their freshly baked pastries, indulging in hearty meals, or enjoying a perfectly balanced cup of coffee, Artisan Blend promises a delightful culinary experience tailored for Nairobi's discerning food and coffee lovers.

What Our Guests Say

Rachel Osewe

"Such an aesthetically pleasing location! I loved it! Easily accessible. You might want to make a reservation if you are a large group especially. I enjoyed the food, though it arrived warm and not hot, it tasted great. The service was fantastic, our waiter was amazing! The staff in general were very helpful and nice."

Wendy Achieng

"Went there during Father’s Day and was there for most of my day. It wasn’t too crowded and quite chill. They use fresh fruits thus the drinks were really yummy. If you are a sweet tooth, I’d highly recommend the blueberry mojito. The chicken curry was also fantastic, it gives a blend between sweet and tangy which was very interesting. Our wait on staff for the day, Immanuela was good, responsive and swift. Be prepared for some of the staff to ask you how you are doing; it’s a very nice initiative but if done so many times it can be a bit overwhelming."

About INTI

INTI, located in the heart of Nairobi, is a sophisticated dining destination that masterfully blends Peruvian and Japanese cuisines into an unforgettable Nikkei experience. Known for its innovative menu and artistic presentation, INTI offers a culinary adventure featuring bold flavors, fresh ingredients, and dishes that are as visually stunning as they are delicious.

Perched with a stunning view of the city skyline, INTI's sleek and modern decor creates an ambiance of refined elegance, perfect for romantic dinners, business lunches, or special celebrations. Whether indulging in expertly crafted sushi, ceviche, or signature cocktails, INTI promises an elevated dining journey that captivates all the senses.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 01/12/2024

Welcome to Nairobi Feast. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy as you discover restaurants and make reservations through our platform.

Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you provide to us, such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Location data
  • Dining preferences
  • Reservation details
  • User-generated content (reviews, ratings, photos)

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:

  • Facilitate restaurant reservations
  • Provide personalized restaurant recommendations
  • Communicate reservation confirmations and reminders
  • Improve our restaurant discovery and reservation services
  • Analyze user behavior to enhance our platform
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support

How We Share Your Information

We may share your information with:

  • Restaurants to fulfill your reservations
  • Service providers who assist us in operating our website and services
  • Other users when you post public reviews or ratings
  • Law enforcement or other governmental agencies, in compliance with the law

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your personal information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Request a copy of your data

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

About Cafe Cassia

Café Cassia, nestled in the serene neighborhood of Karen, Nairobi, is a charming haven offering a perfect blend of tranquility and exceptional cuisine. Known for its farm-to-table philosophy, the café serves a delightful menu of fresh, locally sourced dishes, from hearty breakfasts and vibrant salads to indulgent desserts and aromatic coffee.

The café's idyllic setting, surrounded by lush greenery and elegant decor, provides a relaxing escape from the hustle and bustle of the city. Whether you're enjoying a leisurely brunch, a quiet coffee date, or a family gathering, Café Cassia offers a warm and inviting atmosphere that celebrates the beauty of nature and the joy of good food.

About Pili

Pili Restaurant, located in the luxurious GTC Mall in Nairobi, offers an exceptional dining experience that celebrates the best of Pan-African and contemporary global cuisines. Known for its innovative dishes and expertly curated menu, Pili combines rich African flavors with modern culinary techniques to create a unique and memorable feast for the senses.

The restaurant’s chic and elegant decor, complemented by impeccable service and a vibrant ambiance, makes it the perfect destination for business lunches, romantic dinners, or celebratory gatherings. With a focus on quality, creativity, and authenticity, Pili Restaurant delivers a dining experience that embodies the spirit of modern African hospitality.

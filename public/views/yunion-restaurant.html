About Yunion

Yunion Restaurant, situated in the prestigious GTC Mall in Nairobi, redefines fine dining with its fusion of contemporary Asian flavors and modern elegance. Known for its innovative menu, Yunion offers a culinary journey that seamlessly blends tradition and creativity, with dishes ranging from expertly crafted dim sum to bold wok specialties and signature sushi.

The restaurant's sleek and sophisticated design, complemented by panoramic views of Nairobi's skyline, creates an ambiance of luxury and refinement. Perfect for intimate dinners, business meetings, or celebratory occasions, Yunion Restaurant delivers a dining experience that is as stylish as it is memorable.

What Guests Say

S.K. Hill

"During my first visit to Nairobi, I stayed near this wonderful restaurant, Yunion, which is located inside of the GTC. I discovered it while walking in Westlands. I decided to visit the restaurant and I fell in love with the place. I love the setting, especially the portion that is outdoors. The view of the surrounding skyline was awe-inspiring. The staff and their service were outstanding. They seemed like the nicest people in the world. The food and drinks were absolutely mouth-watering good. The honey salmon platter and the wings were my favorite. I love the "big head" drink and the mimosas. I visited Yunion six times during my eleven day stay. When I return to Nairobi, I will visit this place again and again. Asante, Yunion!"

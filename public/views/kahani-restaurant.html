About Kahani

Kahani Restaurant in Nairobi is a culinary gem that brings the rich, vibrant flavors of Indian cuisine to life in a contemporary setting. Known for its masterfully crafted dishes, from aromatic curries and flavorful biryanis to sizzling tandoori delights, Kahani offers an unforgettable dining experience that caters to lovers of authentic Indian fare.

The restaurant's elegant decor, inspired by a blend of modern and traditional Indian aesthetics, creates a warm and inviting atmosphere, perfect for intimate dinners, family gatherings, or special occasions. With impeccable service and a passion for excellence, Kahani Restaurant promises a feast of flavors that transports you to the heart of India, right in Nairobi.

What Guests Say

Rachel Osewe

"Such an aesthetically pleasing location! I loved it! Easily accessible. You might want to make a reservation if you are a large group especially. Wendy Achieng

"Went there during Father's Day and was there for most of my day. It wasn't too crowded and quite chill. They use fresh fruits thus the drinks were really yummy. If you are a sweet tooth, I'd highly recommend the blueberry mojito. The chicken curry was also fantastic, it gives a blend between sweet and tangy which was very interesting.

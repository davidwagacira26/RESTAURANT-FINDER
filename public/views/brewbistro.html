About Brew Bistro Fortis

Brew Bistro Fortis in Westlands, Nairobi, is a vibrant culinary destination that combines the art of craft brewing with exceptional dining. Known for its lively atmosphere and innovative menu, Brew Bistro offers a unique experience that caters to food enthusiasts and beer lovers alike.

The restaurant boasts an impressive selection of house-brewed beers, complemented by a diverse menu that features both local and International cuisines. From gourmet burgers and wood-fired pizzas to sophisticated main courses, Brew Bistro Fortis ensures there's something to satisfy every palate. With its stylish interiors, spacious outdoor seating, and regular live music events, it's the perfect spot for everything from casual dinners to lively nights out.
